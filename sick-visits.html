Sick Visits

Is Your Child Feeling Under The Weather?

When your little one isn’t feeling well it’s important that they get the proper medical care. Dealing with a sick child is not fun and unfortunately, your child will get sick at some point. It's important that you have our team who can provide your child with immediate, thorough care to help them feel better quickly.

Symptoms And Conditions That Will Require A Same-Day Sick Visit:

Children are prone to many illnesses. They often have various symptoms or discomfort. These are just a few reasons to contact us. However, if you're concerned about or are questioning any symtponms your child may have, then it's time to call our office.

  • Cough
  • Vomiting
  • Fever
  • Diarrhea
  • Ear Infections
  • Sore Throat
  • Abdominal Pain
  • Minor trauma
