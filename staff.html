Our Staff

Meet Our doctors and staff

Our medical staff is highly trained and educated to provide the best care for your child. Keep your child on the right track for a healthy childhood with services from our wonderful staff. All our pediatricians are board-certified and are members of the American Academy of Pediatrics®. From newborn visits and immunizations to school and sports physicals, acute care, illness treatments, our professional team is committed to serving your young ones' health care needs.


Every child deserves the best possible medical care. At Honeygo Pediatrics, our team of caregivers partners with parents to help raise healthy children, both physically and emotionally. Each member of our team is dedicated to the health and well-being of our patients. We strive to provide quality, compassionate care in a warm, friendly environment.

Wieslawa D. Wisinski, MD

Board Certified Pediatric and Adolescent Specialty

Dr. Wieslawa D. Wisinski, M.D. is a Board Certified Pediatrician, a physician specializing in serving medical care for children of all ages.

With over 30 years of experience as a pediatrician, she offers extensive healthcare for babies, kids, and teenagers in the greater White Marsh, Perry Hall, Towson, Baltimore area, and beyond.

Dr. Wisinski earned her medical degree from Jagiellonian University Medical School, Poland. She completed her internship and pediatric residency at the University of Maryland.

After her residency, Dr. Wisinski worked for several years as a pediatrician at Johns Hopkins Bayview ER. She later joined Med Star Pediatrics at White Marsh and now, for more than 20 years, she serves her pediatric patients at Honeygo Pediatrics in White Marsh.

She is certified by the American Board of Pediatrics.

Languages spoken: English and Polish

Christina Green Halley, NP

Board Certified Registered Nurse Practitioner

Christina Halley graduated in 2004 from Stevenson University with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing.

She has been a Pediatric Nurse Practitioner (PNP) at Honeygo Pediatrics since 2013.

Yueun Lee, FNP

Board Certified Family Nurse Practitioner

Yueun Lee is a board-certified family nurse practitioner. She originally received a bachelor's degree in nursing from South Korea. Yueun practiced as a pediatric hemato oncology transplantation nurse for pediatric patients with leukemia at Seoul National University Hospital for 4 years, which is the top ranked in the country. She was also a researcher at the Korean National Tuberculosis Association. Yueun then pursued her career in Advanced Nurse Practitioner and completed a master of science in nursing with a specialty in family nurse practitioner at the university of texas at Austin.

A registered nurse and family nurse practitioner in the state of Texas and Maryland, Yueun is a member of the American Association of Nurse Practitioners.

Throughout her career, Yueun has worked at internal and family medicine clinics across Texas & Maryland, providing excellent care and expert assessment and treatment. She has experience caring for patients ranging from Newborns to geriatric populations and is passionate about preventative care and doing her part to maintain a healthy community.

Languages spoken: English and Korean

Samantha Stokes, PNP

Board Certified Pediatric Nurse Practitioner

Samantha Stokes is a board-certified Pediatric Nurse Practitioner specializing in primary care. She has close to ten years of experience caring for the pediatric population in various nursing roles. She completed her undergraduate education at the Community College of Baltimore County and Towson University earning a Bachelors in Science of Nursing.

She then went on to work in the Pediatric Emergency Department at the Johns Hopkins Children's Center as a registered nurse for over five years. During this time she became certified as a pediatric emergency room nurse and as an instructor for the Emergency Nurses Association’s Emergency Nursing Pediatric Course for local hospitals.

Sam proceeded to complete a Masters of Science in Nursing at Drexel University attending their Pediatric Nurse Practitioner program. She is certified by the Pediatric Nursing Certification Board and is excited to bring her experience in pediatrics, caring for newborns to young adults, to Honeygo Pediatrics.

Languages spoken: English

Emma Canto-Vaught, FNP

Board Certified Family Nurse Practitioner

Emma Canto-Vaught is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner with a special interest in the pediatric population. She has been in the medical field for 12 years caring for patients of all ages, beginning as a medical assistant in Dermatology, Internal Medicine, followed by Obstetrics and Gynecology.

She completed her undergraduate education at the Community College of Baltimore County and Towson University earning a Bachelors in Science of Nursing. She then went on to work in Labor and Delivery at GBMC for over 6 years. She holds a certification in neonatal resuscitation, obstetric monitoring, and advanced life support.

Emma proceeded to complete the Family Nurse Practitioner program at Purdue Global University obtaining a Masters of Science in Nursing. She brings her love of newborn care and dermatology to Honeygo Pediatrics and is delighted to share this with our patients and families!

Languages spoken: English
